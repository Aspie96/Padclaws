Relays

Padclaws is a Nostr client, which you can use to communicate with any number of Nostr relays of your choice.

Nostr relays are controlled independently by third parties. The Admin does not determine the purpose or means of any action (including the processing of personal data) by third-party relays and is not the data controller for the processing performed by relays.

The Padclaws interface may inform you of the URI of some known relays. This does not indicate any endorsement of those relays nor any relationship between the Admin and these relays.
